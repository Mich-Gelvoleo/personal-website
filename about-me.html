B O O K S

My parents are more lenient on buying books than toys as I grew up. The room is always crammed with bookshelves because I used to ask them for a new book every time we go to the malls. The following books at the side are one of the best books I have read and will recommend to anyone I met. The middle one is Malonzo and Victoria's collaboration of what happened to the country after contact with engkantos. Lastly, 1Q84 is Murakami's best work about another dimension with two moons.

Niru Kajitsu x WOOMA - KALMIA

KALMIA is a prequel to SHAMA which also surrounds the main character in this video. What I like about WOOMA's work is the way they utilize a specific color palette to convey the expression of anger, hope, and redemption. Niru Kajitsu is also great on composing the song as it leads the listener to what will happen to Hanna as she is captured by Gui. NIGHT IN THE WOODS

This is an adventure game with a slow burn. It really encourages the player to explore every nooks and crevices to further understand the story it has to present. What made NiTW memorable is that I had so much fun talking to different characters in the game and it also feels like real life with their characterization. I think what me relate to this so much is that I felt like I am Mae. I am in the point of my life where I felt like I am unsure of what to do.
