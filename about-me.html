ABOUT ME

B O O K S

My parents are more lenient on buying books than toys as I grew up. The room is always crammed with bookshelves because I used to ask them for a new book every time we go to the malls. The following books at the side are one of the best books I have read and will recommend to anyone I met. The first book from the left is Marquez' magnum opus and narrates the story of the Buendia Family as years passed. The middle one is Malonzo and Victoria's collaboration of what happened to the country after contact with engkantos. Lastly, 1Q84 is Murakami's best work about another dimension with two moons.

"Get those bad thoughts out of your head. You’re going to be happy."

- Jose Arcadio Buendia, One Hundred Years of Solitude
